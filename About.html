FAQ(Frequently Asked Questions)



What services do you offer?
We provide core services such as custom website development, e-commerce solutions, Website Framework and Design, Website Management, etc.
How does your web development process work?
1) Following the consultation, we construct a website framework tailored to the client's needs.
2) We choose an optimal design that promotes the client's business growth.
3) After finalizing the website framework and design, we initiate development according to the website's category.
4) Upon completing the website development, we proceed with the domain and hosting setup.
5) The client's website is then prepared for delivery.
What technologies do you work with?
We work with various different languages, Framework and Platforms like,
Figma,
WordPress,
HTML CSS JavaScript.
Do you provide website maintenance and support services?
Our services include continuous maintenance, updates, and support following the website's launch.
How long does it take to complete a typical project?
The time required to complete a project varies depending on the type of service the client has selected and their vision for the website. Ensuring the website's proper functionality is also crucial. However, we are committed to delivering the client's website at the earliest opportunity.
Can you help with website hosting and domain registration?
Yes, we offer both website hosting and domain registration services. The choice of domain name and the type of registration depend on the client's preferences.
Do you create responsive websites?
Certainly, we develop responsive websites for all our clients. Having a responsive website that adapts to various devices is crucial.
What happens if I encounter issues after the website is launched?
WAC consistently addresses any issues or bugs that may arise post-launch. WebsiteAtClic offers ongoing Website Management Services even after the website goes live.
